## The Framework

### Step 1: Identify Your Domain Expertise

**What industry do you know deeply?**
- B2B sales (our example: industrial distribution)
- Real estate
- Financial services
- Healthcare
- Legal
- Construction
- Logistics
- Manufacturing
- Retail
- Education

**You need:** 5-10 years in the industry, understanding of real pain points

### Step 2: Find Expensive, Repetitive Problems

**Ask yourself:**
- What do I spend hours on that could be automated?
- What information is scattered that should be centralized?
- What decisions require expertise that AI could augment?
- What mistakes cost thousands of dollars?
- What tribal knowledge exists only in people's heads?

**Example (Industrial B2B Sales):**
- Preparing for customer meetings (30-60 min of research per call)
- Territory planning across huge regions (poor coverage, wasted travel)
- Competitive intelligence (flying blind, inconsistent)
- Account prioritization (gut feel, not data-driven)

### Step 3: Build Solutions for YOURSELF First

**Use Claude Code to create:**
- Customer intelligence dashboard
- Territory optimization tool
- Competitive tracker
- Whatever solves YOUR problem

**Why yourself first:**
- You understand the problem intimately
- Immediate validation (does it actually help?)
- No sales required (you're the customer)
- Proof of concept before investing heavily

**Time investment:** 20-40 hours to build useful tools

### Step 4: Measure Impact

**Track metrics:**
- Time saved per week
- Revenue increase
- Better decisions made
- Errors prevented
- Efficiency gains

**Example results:**
- Customer intelligence: Save 5-10 hours/week on research
- Territory planning: 30% better coverage, 20% less travel
- Competitive intelligence: 15% higher win rate
- Career roadmap: Clear path to promotion

**Total value to you:** $50-100K+ annually in productivity gains

### Step 5: Package as Product

**The realization:**
- If this saves YOU $50K/year...
- It saves everyone in your role $50K/year
- There are 100,000+ people in similar roles
- They'd pay $5-10K/year for this solution
- **That's a $500M-1B market**

**How to package:**
- Clean up your tools
- Make them configurable (not just for you)
- Build simple UI/interface
- Create onboarding
- Write documentation

**From personal tool ‚Üí SaaS product**

### Step 6: Validate with 10 Customers

**Find your first customers:**
- People in your network
- Industry peers
- LinkedIn outreach
- Industry forums/conferences

**Pricing:**
- Start: $200-500/month (pilot pricing)
- Prove ROI in 30-90 days
- Get testimonials and case studies
- Iterate based on feedback

**Goal:** $5-10K MRR, prove people will pay

### Step 7: Build the Business

**Decision point: Bootstrap or Venture?**

**Bootstrap Path:**
- Grow profitably from customer revenue
- Stay lean (you + 1-2 people)
- Reach $1-3M ARR in 2-3 years
- Take $500K-1M/year in distributions
- Exit for $50-150M or keep as cash machine

**Venture Path:**
- Raise $1-3M seed round
- Hire team (10-15 people)
- Grow fast to $10M+ ARR in 3 years
- Raise Series A ($10-20M)
- Exit for $200-500M in 5 years

**Both create wealth. Choose based on goals and risk tolerance.**
